The Story Behind Diwali

Diwali marks the return of Lord Rama to Ayodhya after 14 years of exile, symbolizing the victory of good over evil.

It is also celebrated as the victory of Lord Krishna over Narakasura and the worship of Goddess Lakshmi for prosperity.

How We Celebrate Diwali

Diwali is a five-day celebration that includes lighting diyas, bursting fireworks, and worshiping Goddess Lakshmi.

Families decorate their homes, share sweets, and wear new clothes to celebrate the occasion.

Diwali Around the World

Diwali is celebrated across the globe in countries like Nepal, Malaysia, and Singapore.

It brings communities together in joy, spreading light and happiness everywhere.
